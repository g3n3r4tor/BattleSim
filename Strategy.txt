Strategy for BattleSim:


If enemy moves = attack if within range?
if attack not within range and stamina not full = rest
If enemy attacks and your health is lower = dodge.
If you cannot dodge = defend.
if enemy attacks and your health is higher = attack back. 
If enemy rests and you are in range = attack.
If enemy defends = rest.
If stamina < move u want to do = rest.
